    Mission Statement

      Through digitalization and innovation in academic design and delivery, T.I.P. students, faculty, staff, and industry partners work together in both traditional and online/
      flexible learning to transform our students to achieve optimal student outcomes:.

      • Full competence in their fields of study
      • Ability to apply their competencies, mindsets, and values to serve the needs of industry or to pursue technopreneurship to solve societal problems.
