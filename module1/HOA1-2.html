Vanjo Merano: Pork Adobo with Potato Recipe

source

Adobo is a populatr Filipino Pork Recipe that is considered as a Filipino signature dish.

Creator of PanlasangPinoy.com. Vanjo Merano goal is to introduce Filipino Food and Filipino Cuisine to the rest of the world. This recipe blog was the first step that he took.

- Vanjo Merano

Ingredients:

  • pork sliced into cubes: 2 lbs
  • potatoes cubed: 2 medium
  • white vinegar: 1/4 cup
  • soy sauce: 1/2 cup
  • crushed garlic: 5 cloves
  • dried bay leaves: 3 pieces
  • whole pepper corn: 1 tbsp
  • water: 1 cup
  • cooking oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Salt to taste 1/2 cup

Preparation:

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Saut√© the garlic for 30 seconds.
  3. Add the pork and whole peppercorn. Cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
  4. Pour-in soy sauce and water.
  5. Add the bay leaves and let boil. Simmer (covered) for about 40 to 60 minutes. Note: Add water if needed
  6. Put-in the vinegar and allow re-boiling.
  7. Add the potatoes. Simmer for 10 to 12 minutes.
  8. Add salt.
  9. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!

Cooking Tips:

  • chicken may be used as an alternative if you do not want pork.
