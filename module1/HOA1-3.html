Welcome to Roselia

We are Roselia, a japanese all-girl bands that is from the BanG Dream! franchise game with the group consisting of five members. Yukina (vocals), Hikawa Sayo (guitar), Imai Lisa (bass), Udagawa Ako (drums), and Shirokane Rinko (keyboard). While they came from fiction they too have a real life counter part which is also as talented and popular which are Aiba Aina (Minato Yukina), Kudou Haruka (Hikawa Sayo), Nakashima Yuki (Imai Lisa), Sakuragawa Megu (Udagawa Ako), Shizaki Kanon (Shirokane Rinko).
