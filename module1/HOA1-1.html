Minecraft Bosses

Wither Storm

Only appears on Minecraft story mode, it is a very destructive alteration of the normal wither only using a command block at the center. It is nearly immortal against all attacks and needed a very specific set of armaments making it dangerous.

Difficulty: ✰✰✰✰✰

source: https://minecraft-archive.fandom.com/wiki/Wither_Storm

Warden

Appears in the recent updates of Minecraft at Caves and Cliffs, it is dangerous to go against face to face even with end game gears. Going agains this mob requires strategy making use of its ability of it being sensitive to noise.

Difficulty: ✰✰✰✰

source: https://minecraft-archive.fandom.com/wiki/Warden

Ender Dragon

Located in the end dimension, it is the most known boss in minecraft making it easier to most adept and senior players having known the mechanics as well as the easier to understand rules on how to properly fight against it.

Difficulty: ✰✰✰

source: https://minecraft-archive.fandom.com/wiki/Ender_Dragon

Wither

A nether boss considered traditionally as the second boss in minecraft. The difficulty varies from the type of minecraft being played with such that fighting against it in Java are easier while Bedrock is considerably difficult.

Difficulty: ✰✰✰✰

source: https://minecraft-archive.fandom.com/wiki/Wither

Elder Guardian

A boss that spawns underwater at the ocean monuments, the elder Guardian it simply a buffed up version of a guardian mob with it inflicting Mining Fatigue against the player making escape upon encounter difficult but is fairly easy to fight against when properly prepared.

Difficulty: ✰✰

source: https://minecraft-archive.fandom.com/wiki/Elder_Guardian
